About Net Pay Advance

Fast, friendly online loans—backed by real people who care

Since 2007, Net Pay Advance has helped thousands of people handle emergency expenses with quick online loans.

Access to money in tough times shouldn’t be complicated, delayed, or judgmental. Our mission is simple: make online lending easier, more respectful, and rooted in real human support.

Our story

Net Pay Advance was born from a simple idea: people deserve financial support without shame or red tape. Our founder saw how many were excluded by traditional banks and wanted to create something better and easily accessible.

Over the years, our products have evolved—but our purpose hasn’t changed: to help people move forward, not fall behind.

Meet the team

We’re a crew of loan experts and finance professionals—all working together from our main office in Wichita, Kansas. When you call or email us, you’re speaking with a real person who’s here to help.

Brian J. Staver, President

After serving in the military, Brian began his journey as a life-long entrepreneur. Leading companies in retail manufacturing before pivoting to the online lending space in 2007 and expanding into merchant processing in 2018. Brian’s goal is to help others and motivate his employees to do the same through hard work, perseverance, and innovation.

Daniel Mackenthun, COO / CFO

Daniel joined the team in 2023 as a consultant and ultimately transitioned to full-time in 2024. With over 28 years of experience in financial services, Daniel helps lead the organization through his expansive knowledge and skillset. Empowering others with his logical approach to business objectives and helping the company thrive.
